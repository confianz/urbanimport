Odoo Multichannel EBay Connector

"Bridging the difference of Odoo with EBay!"
Note:
Odoo Multichannel Sale is the base module which needs to be installed first for the Odoo EBay Connector to work properly.

Odoo Multichannel EBay Connector is the apt solution for connecting two whole different platforms.

Whether it is seller data(like orders and catalogs) or inventory you can manage it all with the help of the module. It facilitates synchronization, import and export as per your business requirements.

eBay also consistently ranks as one of the biggest U.S. online companies based on market cap, which was almost 33.5 billion U.S. dollars as of September 2019.

Hence, selling out your products in the eBay marketplace can really boost your sale. It offers you great exposure to make a remarkable presence of your products worldwide.

However, you need to manage all the data efficiently of your marketplace store account.

Odoo never stays behind when talking about flawless backend operation management. Moreover; now, it is no big deal to connect one or more eBay marketplace stores with a single Odoo instance.

You can also connect to multiple e-commerce and marketplaces to your single Odoo instance with the help of Odoo Multi-channel Sale.

Connect Your Odoo With Multiple EBay Seller Accounts

Odoo Multichannel EBay Connector perfectly works in such situations. You can simply connect one or more EBay Seller Accounts to your Odoo; and manage its data in your single Odoo Instance.

The need of managing the data in a centralised way cannot go unnoticed as it helps to track the data efficiently. When data is decentralized and disorganized, it becomes an obstacle rather than an asset.

Importing Catalogs in Odoo

You can import Data like Products, Categories, attributes, as well as attribute values from EBay to Odoo.

The module makes the Import process quick and easy. You can import data manually or via cron scheduler. The data is stored in the form of feeds in Odoo and then automatically creates data on the basis of the settings.

Mapping is created in the module to avoid duplicity of the data.

Exporting Catalogs in Odoo

Odoo Multichannel EBay Connector has made the export process really convenient. Now, you can export products along with its category from Odoo to EBay.

You can export products directly from the specific product form.

Importing Orders in Odoo

You can import Orders along with the data associated with it from EBay to Odoo. The import function is simplified with the help of module Odoo Multichannel EBay Connector.

Orders statuses like cancel, shipment and invoice are automatically imported at the same time.

Moreover, data like customers and their addresses, taxes, delivery methods, payment methods, currency is also imported along with it.

Key Features

Below is the detailed list of features from Odoo MultiChannel EBay Connector

Importing Products in Odoo

  • Products are manually imported in bulk
  • You can also import products via cron scheduler
  • Product details (like category, attributes, attribute values) are imported along with the products

Importing Orders in Odoo

  • Orders are manually imported in bulk
  • You can import orders via cron scheduler
  • Order details (like customers and their addresses, taxes, delivery methods, payment methods, currency) are imported along with the orders
  • Order statuses (like cancel, shipment and invoice) are also imported along with the orders

Importing Categories in Odoo

  • You can manually import categories in Odoo Or, can also import it via cron scheduler

Importing Business Policies in Odoo

  • Business Policies are manually imported in bulk

Importing Shipping Methods in Odoo

  • Shipping Method are manually imported in bulk

Manually Export Products from Odoo

  • You can export products on bulk Or, export selected products

Importing categories in Odoo

  • You can manually import categories in Odoo Or, can also import it via cron scheduler

Real time Synchronization

  • You can sync order status like shipment, and Inventory from Odoo to EBay in real-time.

Manually Map Orders Statuses

  • You can manually map order statuses from Odoo end
  • Fill the corresponding Order status of EBay and Odoo in the Order Status table

Managing Synchronization History

  • All the synchronization history is managed in Odoo

Enhanced Dynamic Dashboard

  • Introduced a dynamic dashboard to track the data of EBay to your Odoo.
  • Import/Export the data of any channel directly from the dashboard.
  • Well-designed Donut Chart and Line Graph to analyze the data.
  • The dashboard also offers Clickable tabs and data cards.

Clickable Data Cards for the Details

  • View the imported Product, Category, Order and Customers in the Tabs
  • The tabs are clickable and direct you to the respective mapping, feeds as well as sync history.

Line Graph to Analyze the data

  • Drop down to Select the Data Type (Order, Product, Category and Customer)
  • Track the data the Line Graph

Donut Chart to Analyze the data

  • Drop down to Select the Data Type (Order and Product)
  • Track the data of connected EBay instance from the chart
